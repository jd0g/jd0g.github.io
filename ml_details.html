Device Component Failure Predictive Potential

An Hypothesis-Based Analysis

This is a framework to quickly identify the predictive potential of failing device components using hypthesis testing statistics.

  • Finds a division in failure data using clustering and iterates through t-tests to determine how far in advance a reasonable prediction is possible.
  • The top chart supports a reasonable certainty of failure prediction approximately 4 days prior (at a significance of .05).
  • The bottom chart shows a clear indication that something is "off" with the component signal starting at about 50 days prior to failure.
  • Notable techniques: K-means clustering, partial least squares discrimination, Student's t-test


UN General Debates NLP Analysis

An Exercise in Natural Language Processing

Here are some insights from the analysis of the United Nations general assembly debates corpus from 1970-2016.

  • The dominant sentiment across the 5 permanent UN members is trust followed by anticipation and fear.
  • For multiple word phrases, human rights is the most used, followed by middle east and nuclear weapons.
  • The cord chart is a beautiful representation of various levels of interest (and disintrest) in global topics.
  • Notable techniques: Linear Discriminant Analysis classification and Rapid Automatic Keyword Extraction.


Targeting Magazine Sales

Through Association Rules

This is an example using association rules to identify magazine categories that are most likely to interest customers based on previous purchase behavior.

  • Lift is commonly used to identify the most powerful association rules as shown in the left chart.
  • The lift matrix indicates the strongest associations in the top left corner (highest lift)
  • The right chart illustrates that garden and novelty.gift are the identified magazines for the top rules along with the corresponding preceding (predictive) magazine categories.
  • Notable techniques: Apriori algorithm sorted by resulting lift
